Our Story

Built for Every Man in the Kingdom

Kingdom Barbers was built on a simple idea: every man deserves a great haircut in a place that feels like his own. We brought modern barbering technique to St. George with a shop that's confident enough to be called a Kingdom, and welcoming enough that you'll bring your kids.

Every barber here is trained in modern cutting and classic barbering. We invest in technique so you get consistent, quality results — not whatever the barber feels like that day. You'll leave looking sharp and feeling like yourself, only sharper.

"Manly and family-friendly. That's the Kingdom way."

We're located on W Sunset Blvd, one of St. George's main corridors. Easy to get to, easy to park, and open late enough that you can come after work on a weekday.
